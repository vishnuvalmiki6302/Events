About Us

Startup Events

Startup Events is a web-based platform designed to showcase upcoming startup-related events, workshops, and networking opportunities for students and aspiring entrepreneurs. Developed collaboratively by a team of B.Tech students from Lovely Professional University, the website serves as a centralized space to discover and stay informed about innovation-driven activities.

The platform features a clean and responsive interface, event-specific details, and easy navigation for users to explore and engage. Our goal is to bridge the gap between students and the startup ecosystem by promoting awareness and participation in events that foster growth, learning, and collaboration.
